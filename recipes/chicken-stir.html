Chicken Stir Fry

Chicken stir fry is a popular dish inspired by Chinese cuisine. Known for its quick preparation and delicious taste, it combines tender chicken with colorful vegetables, cooked rapidly in a hot pan.

  • 1/2 cups of reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of canola oil
  • 1 broccoli head
  • 1 seedless and chopped bell pepper
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless, chicken breast
  • 1/3 cups of cashew nuts
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Below is a recipe to cook a good bowl of Chicken stir fry:

  1. Prepare ingredients

    In a small bowl, whisk soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.

  2. Cook vegetables and chicken

    In a large skillet over high heat, heat oil. Cook broccoli, bell pepper, and garlic, stirring frequently, until softened, about 5 minutes. Add chicken and cook, tossing occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through, about 8 minutes. Stir in cashews; season with pepper.

  3. Finish and season

    Stir in cashews and season with pepper.

  4. Serve

    Serve the dish whiles it is hot.
