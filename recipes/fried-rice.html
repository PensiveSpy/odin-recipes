Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish of Chinese origins. It is by far one of the world's most known chinese cuisine. It is relatively easy to make and very delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 eggs lightly whisked
  • 2 chopped bacon rashers
  • 2 trimmed shallots
  • 1/2 cup of frozen thawed peas
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds
  • Shallots

Below is a recipe to cook a good bowl of Fried rice:

  1. Cook the rice

    Cook the rice in a large saucepan of boiling water for 12 minutes and drain and leave it to cool.

  2. Heat Oil

    Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add eggs and swirl to form an omelette. Cook for 2 minutes and let it rest.

  3. Add other ingredients

    Add bacon to frying pan and cook for 4 minutes. Add carrots and stir for 1 minutes. Add shallots, rice, and peas and cook for 3 to 4 minutes. Add eggs and soy sauce. Sprinkle sesame seeds and top with more shallots

  4. Serve

    Serve the dish whiles it is hot.
