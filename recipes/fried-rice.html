Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish of Chinese origins. It is by far one of the world's most known chinese cuisine. It is relatively easy to make and very delicious.

Ingedients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 eggs lightly whisked
  • 2 chopped bacon rashers
  • 2 trimmed shallots
  • 1/2 cup of frozen thawed peas
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds
  • Shallots
