Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a West African dish with origin in Senegal. The food is eaten for various occasions and it a very tasty meal that is relatively easy to make.

Ingredient:

  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 medium plum tomato
  • 1/2 medium red onion
  • 1 inch piece of fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable or peanut oil
  • 1/2 medium red onion
  • 1/4 cup of tomato paste
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 1/2 cup of curry powder
  • 1 bouillon cube
  • 2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
