Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a West African dish with origin in Senegal. The food is eaten for various occasions and it a very tasty meal that is relatively easy to make.

Ingredient:

  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 medium plum tomato
  • 1/2 medium red onion
  • 1 inch piece of fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable or peanut oil
  • 1/2 medium red onion
  • 1/4 cup of tomato paste
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 1/2 cup of curry powder
  • 1 bouillon cube
  • 2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups of chicken stock

Below is a recipe to cook a good bowl of Jollof Rice

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit
  2. Prepare the pepper stew base

    Add bell peppers, tomato, onion, scotch bonnet or habanero pepper, ginger, and garlic into a blender until smooth. Set it aside.

  3. Fry the onions

    Heat the oil and add onions to fry for 2-3 minutes

  4. Cook the tomato paste

    Add the tomato paste and cook it for 4 to 5 minutes, while stirring frequently.

  5. Cook the pepper stew base
